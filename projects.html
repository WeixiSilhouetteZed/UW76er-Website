  • Optimal Execution with Nonlinear Market Impact: ACTSC972: Finance III final project, graded by Prof. Alexander Schied. The paper and presentation focus on the optimal execution of a large metaorder considering a nonlinear price impact dynamics. Utilizing both integral equation solving techniques, such as Homotopy Analysis Method (HAM), and numerical optimization methods, we can reach rather asymmetric solutions compared to classical VWAP or linear solution. See paper and presentation slides. Check github repo for implementation codes.
